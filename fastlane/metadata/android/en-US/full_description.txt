<p>Khushu is a private Muslim app built to support worship without ads, trackers, or subscriptions.</p>
<p>It brings together prayer times, guided prayer, tasbih, Quran, hadith, and focused study tools in one calm, modern experience.</p>
<p><b>What Khushu offers</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Home screen with prayer times, current prayer state, sun progress, and Islamic month events</li>
<li>Guided prayer flow for users who want a clean rakat-by-rakat companion</li>
<li>Tasbih collections with immersive counters and customizable bead styles</li>
<li>Quran reading with Arabic text, translations, tafsir catalogs, and audio support</li>
<li>Hadith and study sections designed for quiet reading and reflection</li>
<li>Prayer notifications, pre-prayer reminders, and optional extra timings like sunrise and midnight</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Built with care</b></p>
<ul>
<li>No ads</li>
<li>No analytics</li>
<li>No account required</li>
<li>Local-first design where possible</li>
<li>Focused visual design for worship instead of social noise</li>
</ul>
<p>Khushu is still early, but the intention is clear: make a beautiful, respectful tool that helps Muslims return to prayer, dhikr, and study with more presence.</p>
