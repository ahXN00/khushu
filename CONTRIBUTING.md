# Contributing to Khushu•

**Assalamu Alaikum!** 

JazakAllah Khair for your interest in contributing to Khushu. We are building this for the Ummah as a form of *Sadaqah Jariyah*, and we are honored to have you join us.

## 🎨 Our Philosophy
Khushu is built on three core pillars:
1. **Focus:** No distractions, no ads, no trackers.
2. **Beauty:** A "Pure Black" aesthetic designed for OLED screens and spiritual immersion.
3. **Authenticity:** All Quran and Hadith data must come from verified and authentic sources.

## 🛠️ Getting Started
1. **Fork** the repository and clone it locally.
2. Open the project in **Android Studio (Ladybug or newer)**.
3. We use **Kotlin 2.2.10** and **Jetpack Compose**.
4. Familiarize yourself with our design language in `design.md`.

## 📜 Religious Accuracy
If you are contributing to the **Learn** section or modifying any Quran/Hadith assets:
- You **must** provide the source and grade (for Hadith).
- We prefer references from [Sunnah.com](https://sunnah.com) or [Quran.com](https://quran.com).

## 🚀 How to Contribute
- **Bug Fixes:** Feel free to pick up any open issue labeled `bug`.
- **New Features:** Please open a **Feature Request** issue first so we can discuss the design and scope.
- **Design:** We love UI/UX improvements that respect our minimalist aesthetic.

## 🤝 Code of Conduct
Please be respectful and kind in your communications. We are all here to serve the Ummah and earn the pleasure of Allah (SWT).
